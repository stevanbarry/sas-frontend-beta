About

Save a Selfie is an interactive mobile application that has been designed and developed to help members of the public pinpoint the location of emergency equipment in their local community. We need your help! Do you know the location of your nearest AED, First Aid Kit, life ring or Fire Hydrant? Why not help us by taking a selfie with it? – or if you’re shy a photo will do! Help us spread the location of these pieces of life-saving equipment. Photos you upload will be shared with members of the emergency services and also members of the public to raise awareness of the locations of these devices. In an emergency situation seconds count! Your smile could literally save a life!
